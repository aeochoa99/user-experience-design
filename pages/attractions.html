Attractions

Scuba Diving

Dive beneath the turquoise waters of Taniti and discover a world of vibrant coral reefs, tropical fish, and hidden sea caves. Whether you’re a first-time diver or an experienced explorer, our certified guides ensure a safe and unforgettable underwater adventure.

Dolphin Encounter

Meet Taniti’s friendly dolphins up close in this once-in-a-lifetime experience. Learn about these intelligent marine animals, watch them play, and enjoy a guided interaction in a safe and respectful environment that’s fun for all ages.

Spa Experience

Relax and recharge with a soothing spa treatment inspired by island traditions. Enjoy massages, aromatherapy, and natural body treatments using locally sourced oils and ingredients — the perfect way to unwind after a day of adventure.

Guided Island Tour

Discover the heart of Taniti with a local guide who will lead you through lush jungles, scenic viewpoints, and cultural landmarks. Along the way, you’ll hear stories of the island’s history, traditions, and people — offering a deeper connection to Taniti’s rich heritage.

Additional Attractions

Beyond the main adventures, Taniti offers countless ways to explore and unwind. Visitors can hike through lush rainforests, tour the island’s cultural villages, or browse handmade crafts at the local market. Thrill seekers can enjoy ziplining through the jungle or taking a scenic fishing excursion, while those seeking relaxation can sail on a sunset cruise or join a beach bonfire under the stars. No matter your interests, Taniti has something special waiting to be discovered.
